Problems visible at true size: p-3 (12px) padding + 1px border eats ~26px from an already ~113px column. Mode buttons with their own borders and inset shadows create visual noise at this scale. Native range inputs look alien. Controls overflow or become cramped.

Remove section border. Reduce padding to p-2 (8px). Custom 3px slider tracks with 8px thumbs. Flat mode rows (no borders/shadows). Tighter vertical gaps.

Changes: No section border. Padding reduced to 6px/7px. Custom 3px tracks with 8px color-coded thumbs (distortion design language). Mode rows are bare label+value text — zero borders/shadows/backgrounds. Vertical gaps compressed to 1-4px. Works at all 3 breakpoints.
Trade-off: Keeps horizontal slider orientation since the column is too narrow for vertical sliders (would need ~5 sliders in ~74-124px horizontal space).
